Welcome! I finally got around to setting up a personal site — if you're reading this, it's working.

## What This Is

This is a place for me to share my work, document my thinking, and keep a record of projects and ideas as they evolve. I work in Data & AI, so most of what I write will be in that space — but I may venture into other territory as the mood strikes.

## What to Expect

A few things I plan to write about:

- **Technical deep-dives** — ML system design, data pipeline architecture, things I've built and what I learned
- **Paper notes** — quick takeaways from papers I've read (mostly ML/AI)
- **Opinions** — takes on tools, workflows, or industry trends (clearly labeled as such)
- **How-tos** — things I had to figure out and wish someone had written down

I'll aim for quality over quantity. No commitment to a posting schedule.
